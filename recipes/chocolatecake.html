Chocolate Cake

Description

Delicious, simple and easy chocolate cake recipe. Perfect for adding you own touch and creating with diferent fillings!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 2 cups of wheat flour
  • 1 cup of chocolate
  • 1 cup of hot water
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 1 spoon os baking powder

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix all ingredients until smooth except the baking powder;
  2. Combine de baking powder and mix gently;
  3. Transfer the mixture to a greased pan and bake in a preheated oven at 180Â°C, until the house smells like cake.
  4. You can add some filling when the cake get cold
